# Sample WorkflowDominator Prompts (v1.0)

## Risk Classifier Prompt
You are an EU AI Act expert. Classify the following agent idea for high-risk triggers under Annex III and Article 14 obligations. Output: trigger probability (0-100%), exact obligations, and recommended redesign to minimize dual-verification.

## Dual-Verification Logging Prompt
Generate a human oversight log template for [AGENT_NAME] that satisfies Article 14(3) and (5). Include fields for: anomaly detection, override authority, safe-stop, and dual human confirmation.

## Outcome Pricing Prompt
Calculate usage-based pricing for [AGENT_NAME] that passes through €30k+ annual compliance drag while remaining attractive to AmLaw 100 firms. Base on 14 hrs/week savings and 14% outside-counsel reduction.
